Welcome to the File Sync Application

Our application helps you manually synchronize your selected files and folders with your Google Drive account. This ensures that your important data is always available and secure in the cloud.

Key Features

  • Manually select files and folders for synchronization with Google Drive.
  • Syncs files automatically when changes are detected in the selected folders.
  • Runs seamlessly in the background, even when power-saving mode is enabled.
  • Full control over which subfolders or files inside a main folder are excluded from synchronization (e.g., exclude `node_modules`).
  • Secure login using your Google account to authorize access to Google Drive.
  • User privacy is a priority â€“ no one can access your files except you.

How It Works

After installing the application, follow these simple steps:

  1. Launch the application and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Select the files or folders you want to sync with your Google Drive.
  3. Configure advanced settings, such as excluding specific subfolders from synchronization.
  4. Let the application run in the background to automatically keep your files synced.

Privacy and Security

We take your privacy seriously. All file transfers are encrypted, and you have full control over your data. You can access, modify, and delete synchronized files at any time.
